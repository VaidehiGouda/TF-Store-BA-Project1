Current State
We are a store named Today Fashions. In this store men, women and kids' dresses are displayed inside the store in different sections. One dress of each type is displayed on the mannequin, similar dresses of different colour, sizes are kept close folded. Men, women, kids dresses are in separate section in the store. There is one billing desk with 3 counters near the exit(entry/exit). There are 20 staff help customers when they need any information about the dresses or if they want to see a different dress (colour/size/material). There are 3 trial rooms in the store.
There is a chance of a queue to the trial room, to the billing desk during rush hours. During rush hours customers may fall short of staff when assistance is needed. Refolding the cloths after customers sees it may take time. During rush hours it will be a problem to the customers causing delays and unhappy customers. If refolding cloths take more time, the next customer may end up missing that dress and may return unhappy.
After billing when the owner needs the reports, he has to go through the spreadsheet which has entry of each bill, categorise it manually and generate the report - how many orders each day, how many cash/ card/ UPI payments, number of returned, exchanged items. Each of these has to be entered and maintained regularly and monitored at the closing time on a daily basis 

Pain Points: 
During rush hours staff availability and billing becomes little tough. 
Downloading reports rah takes calculations and fetches a good amount of time.
Unavailability of the item customers asked may make the customers unhappy. 
Marketing and competition

Possible Solution: 
Building an app provides users with different options in their phone. It will be easy for them to browse through an app, know what all the store gives, select what they want, add to cart etc. By an app they can decide what they want from the home in their phone.
When they can purchase through app, it is much more convenient for the customers. 
Secure payment and safe customer data builds trust among the customers for TF. It should be easy to browse the app. 
The reports should be available to the owner at a click. He should customise and download the reports through filters wherever he wants.
